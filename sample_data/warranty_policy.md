# NovaTech Warranty Policy

NovaTech products include a limited warranty against manufacturing defects. Wireless Headphones and Smart Keyboard products include one year of limited warranty coverage from the purchase date.

The warranty does not cover accidental damage, cosmetic wear, unauthorized modifications, lost accessories, or consumable parts after normal use.

Customers should create a support ticket when a product stops working unexpectedly. The support team may request troubleshooting details, photos, and proof of purchase.
