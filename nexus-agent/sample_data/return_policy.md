# NovaTech Return Policy

NovaTech accepts returns for eligible products within 30 days of delivery. Returned items must be in good physical condition, include the original accessories, and be accompanied by proof of purchase.

Opened electronics may be inspected before a refund is approved. Products damaged by misuse, unauthorized repair, liquid exposure, or missing serial labels are not eligible for standard returns.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method after the warehouse receives and approves the returned item. Shipping fees are refundable only when NovaTech made a fulfillment error.
